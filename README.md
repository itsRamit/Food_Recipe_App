# Food Recipe App
<img src = "https://github.com/itsRamit/Food_Recipe_App/assets/127865288/f78412f9-3bec-43d6-8138-fbb5bbb58f75" width = "150">

The Food Recipe App is a mobile application developed using Flutter and Dart that allows users to access a collection of delicious food recipes. This app is designed to provide users with detailed recipe information, including cooking instructions, a built-in cook timer, and calorie information for each recipe. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, this app will assist you in preparing mouthwatering dishes with ease.

## Features
Recipe Collection: Browse through a diverse range of recipes for various cuisines, meals, and dietary preferences.

Detailed Recipe Information: Each recipe comes with comprehensive details, including ingredients, step-by-step cooking instructions, and serving portions.

Cook Timer: The app includes a built-in cook timer for each recipe, allowing you to set precise cooking durations. Never worry about overcooking or undercooking your dishes again.

Calorie Information: Keep track of the nutritional content of your meals with the provided calorie information for each recipe. Make informed choices about your diet while enjoying delicious food.

Search and Filters: Easily search for specific recipes or apply filters based on categories, ingredients, or cooking time to find the perfect recipe for any occasion.

Favorites: Mark your favorite recipes for quick access. Create your personalized recipe list to revisit and cook your preferred dishes.(currently this feature is in building)

User-Friendly Interface: The app features an intuitive and user-friendly interface, making it accessible for users of all ages and levels of culinary expertise.
